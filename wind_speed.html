Wind Speed

It is clear to see that wind speeds in the northern hemisphereare are slightly higher than those in the southern hemisphere. For example, max wind speeds in the southern hemisphere are up to 25 mph, while the max wind speeds in the northern hemisphere are above 35 mph! This may be strongly correlated with the fact that this data was gathered in January.
